Frequently Asked Questions

General

Christos.Fashion is a modern Christian apparel brand creating faith-based clothing for believers who want to wear their faith boldly and represent Christ every day. We sell premium t-shirts, hats, and hoodies with bold Gospel-centered designs through our online store.
Christos.Fashion is for every believer who wants their clothing to reflect their faith. Whether you're a student, a professional, an athlete, or a parent — if you want to carry the Gospel message wherever you go, this brand is for you.
Christos (Χριστός) is the Greek word for Christ, meaning "The Anointed One." Every product we make is built around that name, that person, and that truth. It's not just a brand name — it's a declaration.

Shipping & Orders

Orders are produced and fulfilled via Printify. Production typically takes 2–7 business days, and domestic US shipping takes an additional 3–5 business days. Total delivery time is usually 5–12 business days. You'll receive a tracking number once your order ships.
Yes! Orders over $50 qualify for free standard shipping within the United States. Shipping costs for orders under $50 are calculated at checkout based on your location.
Yes. Once your order is fulfilled and shipped, you'll receive a tracking number by email. Use it to follow your order all the way to your door.
Currently our primary focus is US domestic shipping. International shipping options may be available at checkout depending on your location. Contact us at hello@christos.fashion if you have questions about international delivery.

Returns & Quality

Because all products are made-to-order (print-on-demand), we do not accept returns for size exchanges or buyer's remorse. However, if your product arrives defective, damaged, or incorrectly printed, we will replace it at no cost to you. Contact us at hello@christos.fashion within 30 days of delivery with your order number and a photo of the issue.
Yes. We use Printify's premium fulfillment network, which means our products are printed with high-quality inks on soft, durable fabric. The prints are designed to maintain their vibrancy and quality with proper care. Always wash inside-out in cold water for best results.

Sizing

Our t-shirts and hoodies are available in S, M, L, XL, 2XL, and 3XL. The Jesus Saves Snapback is one-size-fits-most with an adjustable snapback closure. Size charts are listed on each product page.
Generally yes. Our shirts fit true-to-size with a regular unisex fit. If you prefer a more relaxed look, we recommend sizing up. Refer to the size chart on each product for exact measurements.

Privacy & Terms

No. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties outside of what is required to fulfill your order. Your email is used only for order updates and, if you opt in, marketing communications. You can unsubscribe at any time.
Yes. All transactions are processed through Printify's secure checkout system with SSL encryption. We never store credit card information on our end.
